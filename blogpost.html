The factors contributing to the box office #1

China has produced a large number of films every year, but the vast majority of the films could not be paralleled with well-regarded films in the international market and the Chinese film market is severely imbalanced. To survive in the market, the Chinese film production company needs to make more efforts to produce high-caliber films and present our traditional culture to other countries.   3. Is the budget directly related with the box office?
